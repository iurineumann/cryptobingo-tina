## Your First Ticket

With your wallet set up and funded, it is time to play.

## Choosing a Room

On the CryptoBingo homepage, you see available rooms:

- **Ticket price:** each room has a different price (e.g., 10 WUF, 50 WUF, 100 WUF)
- **Game mode:** Standard, Fixed, First Full, Golden Ball, Multi Match
- **Max prize:** ceiling defined for that room
- **Players:** how many people are in the room

## Buying Tickets

1. Select a room
2. Choose ticket quantity (1 to 10+)
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
4. Wait ~2 seconds (WAX finality)
5. Done — tickets appear in your account

## Understanding Tickets

Each ticket has 24 numbers + 1 free space in a 5×5 grid. Numbers range from 1 to 75. The combination is deterministically generated from your ticket seed.

**You can see:** your numbers, rooms you joined, match history.

## Tips

- **First match:** start with Standard mode, cheapest ticket
- **Multiple tickets:** increases number coverage but not linearly
- **Balance:** monitor your balance, do not spend more than planned
- **Connection:** verify your wallet is connected before buying

## Next Step

Now that you have tickets, follow the draw and cheer for your numbers!
