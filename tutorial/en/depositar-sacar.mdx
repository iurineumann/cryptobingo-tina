## Deposits

On CryptoBingo, you do not make a traditional "deposit." You simply connect your wallet and buy tickets. The WAX for buying tickets is already in your wallet.

**Steps:**
1. Buy WAX on an exchange
2. Transfer WAX to your wallet (WAX Cloud Wallet or Anchor)
3. Connect to CryptoBingo
4. Buy tickets — WAX goes directly from your wallet to the contract

## Withdrawals (Prizes)

When you win, the prize is automatically sent by the contract to your wallet. No withdrawal request needed.

**What you see:**
- Win notification in the browser
- Wallet balance increases automatically
- Prize transaction visible on block explorer

**Time:** ~2 seconds after draw ends (WAX finality)

## Withdrawals (Manual)

If you want to send WAX from your wallet to an exchange:
1. Copy the exchange's WAX deposit address
2. In your wallet, click "Send"
3. Paste the address, enter the amount
4. Confirm

**Caution:** always verify the address. WAX transactions are irreversible.

## Fees

- **Deposit:** zero (WAX does not charge, only the exchange may charge for withdrawal)
- **Prize:** zero (the contract pays without fee)
- **Withdrawal to exchange:** the exchange may charge a deposit fee
