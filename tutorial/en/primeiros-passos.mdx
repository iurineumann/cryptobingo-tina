## First Time? Let's Go!

Welcome to CryptoBingo — the first on-chain BINGO platform on WAX. This guide takes you from zero to your first win in 7 steps.

## What You Need

Before playing, ensure you have:

1. **A WAX wallet** — [WAX Cloud Wallet](https://wallet.wax.io) (passkey, recommended) or [Anchor Wallet](https://www.anchor.link) (self-custody)
2. **WAX tokens** — to buy tickets (no gas fees; WAX uses staked CPU/NET resources)
3. **A modern browser** — Chrome, Firefox, or Brave

## Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

No wallet? No problem.

- **WAX Cloud Wallet (30 seconds):** go to [wallet.wax.io](https://wallet.wax.io), click "Create Account", choose a 12-character username, use Face ID/Touch ID as passkey
- **Anchor Wallet (5 minutes):** download from [anchor.link](https://www.anchor.link), save seed phrase offline, create WAX account

Start with WAX Cloud Wallet. When you have more tokens, migrate to Anchor for full key control.

→ Full guide: [How to Create Your WAX Wallet](/en/tutorials/criar-carteira-wax)

## Step 2: Get WAX Tokens

You need WAX to buy tickets. Three ways:

- **Centralized exchanges (KYC):** Binance, KuCoin, Gate.io — buy USDT, swap to WAX, withdraw to your wallet
- **Alcor Exchange (no KYC):** connect wallet at [alcor.exchange](https://alcor.exchange), swap tokens directly on WAX
- **Testnet faucet:** free test WAX for practice on testnet

WAX transfers arrive in ~2 seconds.

→ Full guide: [How to Buy WAX](/en/tutorials/comprar-wax)

## Step 3: Connect to CryptoBingo

1. Visit [cryptobingo.bet](https://cryptobingo.bet)
2. Click "Connect Wallet" (top right corner)
3. Approve in your wallet — passkey popup for Cloud Wallet, QR code for Anchor
4. Your WAX username appears in the header — you are connected

## Step 4: Choose a Game Room

Browse rooms in the lobby. Each room shows:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Ticket price** | Entry cost (e.g. 10 WUF, 50 WUF, 100 WUF) |
| **Game mode** | Standard, Fixed, First Full, Golden Ball, Multi Match |
| **Prize pool** | Total WAX available |
| **Players** | Current participants |

**First-timer tip:** start with the cheapest Standard mode room.

## Step 5: Buy Tickets

1. Click the room to enter
2. Choose ticket quantity (1 to 10+)
3. Confirm transaction in your wallet
4. Wait ~2 seconds (WAX finality)
5. Your 5x5 card appears on screen

Each ticket has 24 numbers + 1 free space (numbers 1–75). The combination is deterministically generated from your ticket seed — provably fair from the start.

→ Full guide: [How to Buy Tickets](/en/tutorials/comprar-tickets)

## Step 6: Watch the Draw

Once the room fills or the timer expires, the draw begins:

- Numbers are drawn one by one in real-time
- Every draw uses provably fair hybrid RNG (player seed + server seed + oracle RSA-SHA256)
- Your matched numbers highlight instantly
- Completed patterns trigger win notifications

**RTP 85%:** CryptoBingo returns 85% of all buy-ins as prizes (15% GGR). Prize calculation: `price_paid × base_multiplier × weight/100 × golden_multiplier`.

## Step 7: Claim Your Prize

**Prizes are automatic.** No withdrawal request needed.

1. The contract evaluates all cards against drawn numbers
2. For each winning card, WAX is transferred directly to your wallet
3. A "Winner!" notification appears on screen
4. Check "My Prizes" for complete history

The entire process takes ~2 seconds after the draw ends.

## Troubleshooting FAQ

### I can't connect my wallet

Make sure your wallet extension is installed and unlocked. Refresh the page and try again. For Anchor, verify the QR code scan completed. Clear browser cache if the button does not respond.

### My transaction fails with "CPU limit exceeded"

You need more WAX staked to CPU. Go to [wax.bloks.io](https://wax.bloks.io), connect your wallet, navigate to Wallet → Resources, and stake additional WAX to CPU. Minimum recommendation: 5 WAX.

### I didn't receive my prize

1. Verify you completed a winning pattern (check "My History")
2. Check your wallet balance — the prize may have arrived silently
3. Wait for WAX finality (~2 seconds)
4. Find the transaction on [wax.bloks.io](https://wax.bloks.io)
5. Contact support with your match ID if the problem persists
