## What You Need

Before playing, you'll need:

1. **A WAX wallet** — Anchor Wallet (desktop/mobile) or Cloud Wallet (browser extension)
2. **Some WAX tokens** — to buy tickets and pay transaction fees
3. **A web browser** — Chrome, Firefox, or Brave recommended

## Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

If you don't have a WAX wallet yet:

- **Anchor Wallet**: Download from [greymass.com](https://greymass.com) and create a new account
- **Cloud Wallet**: Install the browser extension and create an account

## Step 2: Get WAX Tokens

You need WAX tokens to buy tickets. On testnet, you can get free test WAX from the faucet. On mainnet, you can buy WAX on exchanges and transfer to your wallet.

## Step 3: Connect to CryptoBingo

1. Visit [test.cryptobingo.bet](https://test.cryptobingo.bet)
2. Click "Connect Wallet" and approve the connection in your wallet
3. Your wallet address will appear in the top right corner

## Step 4: Choose a Game Room

Browse the available game rooms in the lobby. Each room shows:
- Ticket price
- Draw mode (Standard, Fixed, First Full, etc.)
- Current player count
- Prize pool

## Step 5: Buy Tickets

1. Click on a game room
2. Choose how many tickets to buy
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
4. Wait for the draw to begin

## Step 6: Watch the Draw

Sit back and watch as numbers are drawn in real-time. The RNG oracle ensures every draw is provably fair and recorded on the WAX blockchain.

## Step 7: Claim Your Prize

If you win, prizes are automatically credited to your balance. You can withdraw winnings at any time.
