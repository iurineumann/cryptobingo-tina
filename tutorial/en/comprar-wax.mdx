## Before You Buy

You need WAX to buy tickets on CryptoBingo. Here is how to get it.

## Centralized Exchanges

**Binance:** deposit fiat → buy USDT → swap for WAX → withdraw to your WAX wallet

**KuCoin:** similar to Binance, with credit card support

**Gate.io:** direct card purchase, WAX support

Steps:
1. Create exchange account (KYC verification required)
2. Deposit fiat or card
3. Buy USDT
4. Swap USDT for WAX
5. Withdraw to your WAX wallet

## Alcor Exchange (WAX DEX)

Swap tokens directly on WAX without KYC.

1. Connect your WAX wallet at [alcor.exchange](https://alcor.exchange)
2. Choose a pair (e.g., WUF → WAX)
3. Confirm the swap in your wallet
4. Done — WAX in your account

## Transferring to Your Wallet

1. On the exchange, go to "Withdraw"
2. Select WAX
3. Paste your WAX wallet address
4. Confirm — in ~2 seconds tokens arrive

**Caution:** always verify the address. One wrong character = lost funds.

## Common Issues

- **Wrong address:** verify character by character
- **Missing memo:** some exchanges require memo for withdrawal
- **Minimum balance:** WAX requires minimum staking for CPU/NET
- **Withdrawal fee:** the exchange charges a fee (not WAX)
