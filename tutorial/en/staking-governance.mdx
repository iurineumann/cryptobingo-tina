## What is Staking?

Staking is the process of locking your WAX tokens in exchange for network resources (CPU/NET) and rewards. On WAX, staking is required to transact — you do not pay fees, but you need resources.

## Why Stake?

**To play:** without minimum staking, your transactions fail with "CPU limit exceeded"

**To earn rewards:** staking also generates rewards (variable APY)

**For governance:** staked WAX gives voting power on community proposals

## How to Stake

**Via WAX Cloud Wallet:**
1. Log in at wallet.wax.io
2. Go to Settings → Resources
3. Choose WAX amount for CPU and NET
4. Confirm

**Via bloks.io:**
1. Connect your wallet at wax.bloks.io
2. Go to Wallet → Resources
3. Stake WAX to CPU and NET
4. Choose a block producer to vote for (optional for rewards)

## How Much to Stake?

Minimum recommendation for CryptoBingo: 5 WAX to CPU, 1 WAX to NET.

If you play a lot or with multiple wallets, increase to 20 WAX to CPU.

## Unstaking

To withdraw WAX from staking:
1. Go to Wallet → Resources
2. Click "Unstake"
3. Wait 72h (WAX unstaking period)
4. WAX available again

## WAX Governance

**Voting:** staked WAX = voting power. You vote for block producers (21 slots) who maintain the network.

**Proposals:** the WAX community proposes changes — fees, protocol upgrades, new features.

**Staking rewards:** by voting for block producers, you receive rewards proportional to your stake.

## Risks

- **Unstaking period:** staked WAX is locked for 72h when unstaking
- **Volatility:** APY varies with WAX inflation
- **Slashing:** minimal risk on WAX (unlike other PoS chains)

Staking is essential to use WAX. Start small and increase as your activity grows.
