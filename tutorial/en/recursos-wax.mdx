## WAX Has Resources, Not Fees

On WAX, you do not pay gas fees per transaction. Instead, you need resources: CPU, NET, and RAM.

## CPU

Processing time. Each transaction consumes CPU. Your limit is based on how much WAX you have staked.

**Low CPU symptom:** transaction fails with "CPU limit exceeded"

**Solution:** stake more WAX to CPU on your wallet or at [wax.bloks.io](https://wax.bloks.io)

## NET

Network bandwidth. Similar to CPU, you need to stake WAX.

**Low NET symptom:** transaction fails with "NET limit exceeded"

**Solution:** stake WAX to NET

## RAM

Memory to store on-chain data (balance, NFTs, etc.). RAM is bought, not staked.

**Low RAM symptom:** "RAM limit exceeded" when buying a ticket or creating an NFT

**Solution:** buy additional RAM on the market (wax.bloks.io) or on exchanges

## How Much to Stake?

For casual CryptoBingo play:
- CPU: 5-10 WAX staked
- NET: 1-2 WAX staked

If playing with many simultaneous wallets, increase proportionally.

## How to Manage

At [wax.bloks.io](https://wax.bloks.io):
1. Connect your wallet
2. Go to Wallet → Resources
3. Stake WAX to CPU and NET
4. Buy RAM if needed

You can also manage directly in WAX Cloud Wallet (Settings → Resources).
