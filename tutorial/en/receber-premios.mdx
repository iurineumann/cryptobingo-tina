## Auto-Prizes

On CryptoBingo, you do not need to request your prize. The contract pays automatically.

## How It Works

1. The draw ends
2. The contract evaluates all cards
3. For each winning card, the contract calculates the prize:
   `price_paid × base_multiplier × (weight/100) × golden_multiplier`
4. The contract transfers WAX to your wallet
5. Done — the balance appears automatically

## Where to See Your Prizes

- **On the site:** "My Prizes" or "My History" page
- **In the wallet:** balance increases automatically
- **On block explorer:** see the prize transaction at [wax.bloks.io](https://wax.bloks.io)

## Ceiling per Card

Each gametype has a maximum prize ceiling. If the calculation exceeds the ceiling, the value is capped at the contract-defined limit.

## Common Issues

**I did not receive the prize:**
1. Verify you actually won (completed patterns × drawn numbers)
2. Check your wallet — the balance may have increased silently
3. Wait for WAX finality (~2 seconds)
4. Check the block explorer for the transaction

**Prize smaller than expected:**
- May be the gametype ceiling
- Check the Golden Ball multiplier (if applicable)
- Check the completed pattern weight

If the problem persists, contact support with the match ID.
