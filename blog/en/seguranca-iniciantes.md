## Security Starts with You

Blockchain is secure by design, but the security of your account depends on you. 99% of crypto incidents are caused by human error — not technology failure.

## Golden Rule: Your Key, Your Responsibility

In the traditional custodial world (banks), if you lose your password, the bank recovers it. In the crypto world, if you lose your private key or seed phrase, no one can recover it.

**Never share your seed phrase.** It is the master key to everything. No legitimate service, including CryptoBingo, will ever ask for your seed phrase.

**Never type your seed phrase into websites.** Scammers create fake sites that imitate legitimate wallets (WAX Cloud Wallet, Anchor). Always verify the URL.

## Common Scams

**Phishing:** emails or messages that appear to be from CryptoBingo asking for password or seed phrase. We never ask for that.

**Fake sites:** domains similar to cryptobingo.bet (e.g., cryptobing-o.com). Always type the URL manually.

**Fake tokens:** offers of "free" tokens that actually drain your wallet.

**Fake support:** people impersonating support on Discord/Telegram asking for remote access.

**Suspicious airdrops:** nobody gives away free tokens. If it sounds too good to be true, it is.

## Best Practices

**2FA:** enable two-factor authentication on everything that offers it.

**Separate wallets:** use one wallet for gaming (with small balance) and another for savings. The most effective practice.

**Strong passwords:** use a password manager. Never reuse passwords.

**Suspicious browser extensions:** some extensions steal wallet data. Only install what you actually need.

**Updates:** keep browser, wallet, and operating system up to date.

## Security Checklist

Beginner or expert, review this list regularly:

- [ ] Seed phrase stored securely (paper + safe, not digital)
- [ ] 2FA enabled on email and wallet (Cloud Wallet, Anchor)
- [ ] Gaming wallet separate from main wallet
- [ ] CryptoBingo URL verified (cryptobingo.bet)
- [ ] Password manager configured
- [ ] Browser extensions reviewed
- [ ] Browser and OS updated

## Lost Access?

If you lost access to your wallet, unfortunately we cannot help — that is the nature of self-custody. WAX Cloud Wallet allows recovery via passkey or mnemonic. On Anchor, without the seed phrase there is no recovery.

## CryptoBingo Never Asks For

- Your seed phrase or private key
- Your password
- Remote access to your computer
- Deposit to a wallet "for verification"
- Fee to receive prize (prizes are paid automatically by the contract)

Be suspicious of anyone asking for these things, even if they appear to be from CryptoBingo.
