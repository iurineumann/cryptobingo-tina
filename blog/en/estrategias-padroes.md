## Bingo is Luck + Strategy

Unlike what many think, bingo is not just luck. Your choices — how many tickets to buy, which patterns to focus on, when to enter a room — directly affect your expected return.

## Understanding Weights

Each winning pattern has an associated weight, defined in the gametype. The weight determines the prize value relative to the ticket price.

| Pattern | Weight (example) | Prize (ticket 10 WAX) |
|---------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Line | 200 | 20 WAX |
| Column | 200 | 20 WAX |
| Diagonal | 300 | 30 WAX |
| X | 500 | 50 WAX |
| Full card | 2000 | 200 WAX |

Harder patterns (more numbers to complete = higher difficulty = higher weight). The maximum weight per ticket is the ceiling defined in the contract.

## Strategy by Number of Tickets

**1-3 tickets:** focus on line/column patterns (easier to complete). Lower investment, lower prize potential.

**4-10 tickets:** diversify between low patterns (lines) and medium patterns (X, diagonal). Broader number coverage.

**10+ tickets:** maximize high patterns (full card, combinations). With many tickets, the probability of completing high patterns increases.

## The Golden Rule: Don't Concentrate

The most common mistake is buying multiple copies of the same card. This does not increase your number coverage — it only repeats the same combination.

**Right:** 10 cards with different combinations (maximum unique number coverage)
**Wrong:** 10 identical cards (same coverage as 1 card)

## Patterns vs Combinations vs Stages

**Patterns:** line, column, diagonal, X — completed during the draw. Immediate payout.

**Combinations:** multiple patterns on the same card (e.g., 2 lines + 1 column). Additional payout for the set.

**Stages (Multi Match):** match phases with specific patterns. Each stage pays independently.

The ideal strategy depends on the game mode. In Standard, maximize patterns. In Multi Match, balance across stages.

## Bankroll Management

1. **Set a budget:** how much you want to spend per session. Do not exceed it.
2. **Bet consistently:** do not increase bets to "recover" losses.
3. **Diversify rooms:** do not put everything in one match.
4. **Take advantage of bonuses:** if promotions are available, use them to extend your play.

## RTP and Expectation

With 85% RTP, for every 10 WAX wagered, your expectation is to recover 8.50 WAX in the long run. This does not mean you will lose 15% every match — it means the system is calibrated for that average.

## Common Mistakes

**Chasing losses:** increasing bets after losing rarely works and often makes it worse.

**Ignoring weight:** patterns with low weight may not be worth the extra ticket cost.

**Playing without understanding the rules:** each room may have different settings. Read the rules before buying.

Good strategy does not guarantee winning, but it maximizes your chances within the mathematics of the game.
