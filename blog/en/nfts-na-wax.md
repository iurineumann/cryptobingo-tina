## What is an NFT?

An NFT (Non-Fungible Token) is a unique digital asset on the blockchain. While a cryptocurrency like WAX is fungible (one WAX equals another WAX), an NFT is unique — like a collectible card or a work of art.

On WAX, NFTs are the center of the ecosystem. The blockchain was built specifically to tokenize and trade digital assets with near-zero fees.

## How NFTs Work on WAX

**AtomicAssets Standard:** unlike Ethereum's ERC-721, WAX uses the AtomicAssets standard, which is more efficient and allows:

- Collections (NFT groups)
- Schemas (categories within collections)
- Custom attributes (rarity, name, image, etc.)
- Contract-free creation (AtomicHub handles the logic)

**Near-zero fees:** minting an NFT on WAX costs cents. Transferring is practically free.

**AtomicHub:** the largest marketplace on WAX. Buy, sell, create, and transfer NFTs. Over 30K collections available.

## NFTs in CryptoBingo

CryptoBingo integrates NFTs in three ways:

**1. Bingo Tickets:** each purchased ticket generates an on-chain record linked to your account. Technically not a transferable NFT, but the ownership record is public.

**2. Special Rewards:** in promotional events, players can earn exclusive CryptoBingo NFTs as rewards.

**3. Future Integration:** plans to integrate NFTs as payment methods or bankroll bonuses (e.g., owning an NFT from a specific collection gives ticket discounts).

## How to Create an NFT on WAX

You do not need to be a developer to create an NFT on WAX:

1. Connect your wallet on AtomicHub
2. Create a collection (name, description, image, royalties)
3. Create a schema (define your NFT attributes)
4. Mint your first template
5. Create one or more units of the template

Total cost: less than 1 WAX. Simple as that.

## NFT Safety Tips

**Verify the collection:** fake NFTs exist. Always confirm the collection is verified on AtomicHub.

**Royalties:** creators can set royalties (up to 15% on secondary sales). Check before buying.

**Liquidity:** NFTs can be hard to sell at your desired price. Do not invest more than you can lose.

**Scams:** beware of "free mints" from unknown projects. Suspicious links can drain your wallet.

## AtomicHub vs NeftyBlocks

**AtomicHub:** largest marketplace focused on buying and selling. Clean interface, pack support.

**NeftyBlocks:** creation platform with gamification tools (blinding, burning, crafting). Ideal for creators.

Both integrate seamlessly with WAX Cloud Wallet and Anchor.
