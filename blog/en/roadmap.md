## Living Roadmap

This roadmap reflects our current priorities and is subject to adjustment based on community feedback and market opportunities. Everything we do is public and verifiable.

**New Game Modes**
- [ ] Speed Bingo mode (fast balls, short matches)

- [ ] Private rooms for groups/streamers

**Integrations**

- [ ] Expanded cross-chain bridges
- [ ] Hardware wallet support (Ledger)

## How to Follow Along

Everything we implement follows our ADR (Architecture Decision Records) process in docs/adr/. Every major change has a public document explaining the why, alternatives considered, and the decision.

Additionally:
- On-chain contract: every change is recorded on the WAX blockchain
- Open source: our repository is public
- Changelog: we maintain a record of all versions

Suggestions? The community guides CryptoBingo's direction.
