## What Makes WAX Special

WAX (Worldwide Asset eXchange) is a blockchain built specifically for gaming and digital assets. Unlike general-purpose blockchains like Ethereum, WAX is optimized for what matters in gaming: speed, zero fees, and ease of use.

**Zero gas fees:** players never pay transaction fees. On Ethereum, a simple transaction can cost dollars. On WAX, it is free.

**1.5 second finality:** blocks are produced every 1.5 seconds. Compared to Ethereum's 12 seconds or Bitcoin's minutes, the difference is dramatic.

**Carbon neutral:** since 2021, WAX has been carbon neutral — rare in the blockchain space.

## Numbers That Matter

WAX processes more transactions than any other gaming blockchain:

- **215K daily active wallets** (June 2026) — 5th among gaming chains, 11.98% growth in 30 days
- **570-687 million transactions per quarter** — more than any other gaming chain
- **15 million users** and over 30K dApps

## WAX Ecosystem

Beyond CryptoBingo, WAX hosts some of the biggest blockchain games:

**Alien Worlds:** the most played blockchain game ever, with over 420K monthly active wallets. A decentralized economy where players mine, fight, and govern.

**AtomicHub:** the largest NFT marketplace on WAX. Buy, sell, and create NFTs with near-zero fees.

**NeftyBlocks:** NFT creation platform with gamification tools for creators.

The Cloud Wallet lets anyone create an account in 30 seconds with email and password — no seed phrase, no fee. It is the "invisible blockchain" that requires no technical knowledge.

## WAX-TON Bridge

In 2024, WAX launched a bridge with the TON (Telegram Open Network) blockchain, unlocking access to 900 million Telegram users. This positions WAX as the gateway to mobile-first social gaming.

## Why WAX for CryptoBingo?

Every CryptoBingo draw needs to be fast, cheap, and verifiable. WAX delivers exactly that:

- Draws processed in seconds with on-chain confirmation
- Zero fees for players (no gas needed to buy tickets or receive prizes)
- Fast finality — prizes available almost instantly after the draw
- Mature infrastructure with beginner-friendly wallets

## Honest Risks

WAX has strong dependency on Alien Worlds (which represents a large portion of activity). The chain experienced a DAU decline in Q4 2024, followed by recovery in 2025-2026. The zero-fee model requires users to stake WAX for CPU/NET — something that can confuse beginners.

Despite this, WAX remains the most accessible blockchain for casual gaming, with the most mature ecosystem and the best user experience for beginners.
