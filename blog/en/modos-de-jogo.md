## Bingo with Variations

CryptoBingo is not ordinary bingo — it features eight different game modes, each with its own dynamics, risk, and prize potential. All run on the same on-chain contract, all equally verifiable.

## Standard

The classic mode. Numbers are drawn until someone completes a winning pattern (line, column, diagonal, X, among others). The more tickets you have, the higher your chance.

- **Pace:** normal
- **Prize:** fixed per completed pattern
- **Best for:** all players

## Fixed

Fixed number of balls drawn (e.g., 20 balls). Whoever has the most patterns at the end wins. No surprises — you know exactly how many balls will be drawn.

- **Pace:** predictable
- **Prize:** based on patterns completed after N balls
- **Best for:** strategic players

## First Full

Whoever completes the entire card first wins. High tension — the prize is unique and goes to the first player to fill all 24 numbers (free space included).

- **Pace:** fast
- **Prize:** single pot (size varies by ticket sales)
- **Best for:** thrill seekers

## Golden Ball

A golden ball is randomly drawn among the regular balls. If you have the corresponding number marked, you get a prize multiplier (e.g., 2×, 5×, 10×) applied to the highest weight among your completed patterns.

- **Pace:** normal with surprise
- **Prize:** combined with Golden Ball multiplier
- **Best for:** those seeking bigger prizes

## Multi Match (Stages)

The most advanced mode. The match is divided into stages. Each stage has its own winning pattern. You can win in multiple stages in the same match.

- **Pace:** extended
- **Prize:** cumulative per stage
- **Best for:** experienced players

## Quick Comparison

| Mode | Balls | Winner | Prize | Excitement |
|------|-------|--------|-------|------------|
| Standard | Variable | Multiple | Fixed | Medium |
| Fixed | Fixed (e.g., 20) | Multiple | Fixed | Low |
| First Full | Variable | Single | Pot | High |
| Golden Ball | Variable | Multiple | ×Multiplier | High |
| Multi Match | Variable | Multiple | Cumulative | Medium |

## Basic Strategy by Mode

- **Standard/Fixed:** balance between ticket quantity and patterns
- **First Full:** maximize number coverage — diverse cards > many copies of the same
- **Golden Ball:** more tickets = higher chance of having the golden number
- **Multi Match:** focus on the current stage but watch the full board

## Prize Limit

In all modes, the maximum prize per ticket is capped by the ceiling defined in the contract for that gametype. The calculation follows: `price_paid × base_multiplier × (weight/100) × golden_multiplier`.
