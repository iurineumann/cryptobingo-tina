---

## 1. Data Controller

The data controller is CryptoBingo B.V., registered in Curaçao. Our Data Protection Officer (DPO) can be reached at dpo@cryptobingo.bet.

## 2. Data We Collect

We collect: wallet address (WAX pseudonymous identifier), email (if provided), IP address, device fingerprint, cookies, and transaction history on the WAX blockchain.

## 3. Purpose and Legal Basis

Your data is processed for: platform operation and game execution (legitimate interest); AML/KYC compliance (legal obligation); marketing with your consent; and analytics to improve services (consent).

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data with: blockchain networks (WAX — pseudonymous data); analytics providers (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity — with consent); and KYC/AML service providers as required by law.

## 5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and LGPD, you have the right to: access, correct, delete, and port your data; withdraw consent; and lodge complaints with your local data protection authority.

## 6. Blockchain Disclaimer

On-chain transactions are pseudonymous and immutable by design. We cannot delete or modify data recorded on the WAX blockchain.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for 5 years (AML obligation) or until you request deletion, whichever is longer. Blockchain records persist indefinitely.

## 8. Cookies

Our Cookie Consent platform allows you to manage preferences. Necessary cookies are always active; analytics and marketing cookies require your consent.

## 9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards through standard contractual clauses.

## 10. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact dpo@cryptobingo.bet.
