---
title: Dispute Resolution
description: Our process for resolving complaints and disputes.
---

## 1. Contact Channels

Disputes can be submitted via: email to support@cryptobingo.bet, or through our ticket system on the Platform.

## 2. SLA

We acknowledge all complaints within 24 hours. Resolution target is 7-14 business days.

## 3. Escalation

Unresolved complaints escalate: Support Agent → Compliance Officer → Mediation.

## 4. Game Result Disputes

Game result disputes must be submitted within 7 days of the game's conclusion.

## 5. Technical Errors

In case of technical errors, blockchain forks, or network attacks, we reserve the right to void affected games and refund tickets.

## 6. Governing Law

Disputes are governed by Curaçao law. Unresolved disputes may be referred to binding arbitration.

## 7. Language

English is the preferred language for disputes. Portuguese is also accepted.
