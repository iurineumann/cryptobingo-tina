## 1. Policy Statement

CryptoBingo is committed to preventing money laundering and terrorist financing. We comply with applicable AML/CTF regulations.

## 2. Compliance Officer

A designated Compliance Officer oversees our AML program. Reports can be directed to compliance@cryptobingo.bet.

## 3. Risk Assessment

We conduct risk assessments covering: product risk, customer risk, geographic risk, and delivery channel risk.

## 4. KYC/CDD

Know Your Customer procedures include: identity verification, PEP screening, sanctions list checks (OFAC, UN, EU), and beneficial ownership verification.

## 5. Transaction Monitoring

We monitor transactions for suspicious patterns, including: structuring, rapid deposits and withdrawals, and transactions from high-risk jurisdictions.

## 6. Reporting

Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) are filed with relevant authorities as required by law.

## 7. Record Keeping

All AML-related records are kept for a minimum of 5 years as required by FATF recommendations.

## 8. Training

All staff receive annual AML training covering: identification of red flags, reporting procedures, and regulatory updates.

## 9. Crypto-Specific Measures

We employ blockchain analytics to detect: mixer/tumbler usage, privacy coin transactions, and chain-hopping patterns.
