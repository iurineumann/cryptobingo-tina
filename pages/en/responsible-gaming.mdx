## 1. Our Commitment

CryptoBingo is committed to promoting responsible gambling. We provide tools to help you stay in control of your gameplay.

## 2. Deposit Limits

You can set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits through your account settings.

## 3. Loss Limits

Set limits on how much you can lose within a specified period.

## 4. Session Timers

Configure reminders to notify you of your playing time.

## 5. Time-Out (Cooling-Off)

Take a break from 24 hours to 30 days. Your account will be temporarily suspended and cannot be reactivated during this period.

## 6. Self-Exclusion

For extended breaks, self-exclusion options are available for 1, 3, or 5 years. Withdrawals are processed before self-exclusion takes effect.

## 7. Warning Signs

Signs of problem gambling include: spending more than you can afford; chasing losses; lying about gambling; neglecting work or family; and borrowing money to gamble.

## 8. Self-Assessment

Take our confidential self-assessment questionnaire to evaluate your gambling habits.

## 9. Help Resources

If gambling is causing problems, seek help:
- Gamblers Anonymous: www.gamblersanonymous.org
- BeGambleAware: www.begambleaware.org
- GamCare: www.gamcare.org.uk
- Jogadores Anônimos (Brazil): www jogadoresanonimos com br

## 10. Underage Gambling

The Platform is strictly for users 18+. Underage gambling is prohibited. If we suspect a user is underage, we will suspend the account and refund deposits.
