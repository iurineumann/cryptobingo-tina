title: Bug Bounty Program
description: Responsible disclosure program for security researchers.
---

## 1. Scope

In scope: smart contracts (WASM), API endpoints, worker-game-loop, frontend authentication. Out of scope: social engineering, DoS attacks, self-XSS, already-known issues, third-party infrastructure.

## 2. Rewards

Critical: Legendary NFT + $BINGO tokens; High: Epic NFT + $BINGO tokens; Medium: Rare NFT; Low: Uncommon NFT; Note: public acknowledgment and Hall of Fame.

## 3. Disclosure Process

Report via encrypted email to security@cryptobingo.bet. We acknowledge within 24 hours. Triage within 48 hours. Fix timelines: Critical: 24h, High: 72h, Medium: 7d, Low: 14d. Public disclosure after 90 days.

## 4. Hall of Fame

Top researchers are featured on our Hall of Fame page with their handle and the NFTs earned.

## 5. Important

Only test on testnet. Do not access or modify other users' data. Follow coordinated disclosure guidelines.
